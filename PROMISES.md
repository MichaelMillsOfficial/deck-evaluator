# Promises to You

## Your data stays with you

No database. No server-side storage. When you close the tab, everything's gone — nothing is saved between sessions.

No account. No login. No email.

## No AI under the hood

Every tag, score, and metric is deterministic code — rules, pattern matching, and math. The same deck always produces the same result.

External calls are made only to enrich the cards and deck you enter — to fetch card data and power the analysis. Nothing else.

## Use it as much as you want

There's no expensive compute per analysis. Paste as many decklists as you want — the cost is the same as loading any web page.

## Understanding your deck, not just fixing it

The goal isn't to tell you your deck is good or bad. It's to help you understand *why* it plays the way it does — why it runs out of gas, why the mana feels clunky, why certain hands are hard to keep.

The analysis is built to surface the mechanics underneath your decisions, so you can reason about your deck yourself.

## Budget and non-staples are first-class citizens

This tool won't push you toward a subset of "best" cards. When suggestions surface, you'll see paths to both upgrade *and* downgrade — because a $5 card that does 90% of the job is a real option, and this tool should treat it as one.

Playing non-staples and budget builds is valid. The analysis is here to help those decks too.
